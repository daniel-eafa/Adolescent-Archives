Our Motive

During the 2021-2022 school year, a couple of highschoolers read the popular piece of American literature, Americana. In this novel, a Nigerian woman named Ifemelu suffered the struggles of immigrating to America. She often recieved uncalled for comments about hair, accent, and skin color. However, Ifemelu used blogging as a way to escape the real world. She would constantly write blogs that act as an outlet for the frustration she holds in each day. By reading this novel we realized the power of blogs. Some people need something as simple as a blog to get something off their chest and stay in the best mental state.
